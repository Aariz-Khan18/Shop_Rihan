About Malik Tailors

Over 27 Years of Sartorial Excellence

Welcome to Malik Tailors & Fabrics, Datia's premier destination for bespoke menswear. Founded and led by Master Tailor Mr. Malik, we have been proudly serving our community for over 27 years, bringing unmatched dedication, precision, and artistry to every single stitch.

Whether it is a sharp 3-piece suit for a corporate meeting, a regal Indowestern for a wedding, or perfectly fitted daily wear pants and shirts, Mr. Malik's decades of expertise ensure that every garment reflects your unique style. We pride ourselves on housing top-tier fabrics like Raymond, Siyaram, and authentic Italian blends to give you a truly premium experience.
